What is the ArtLibs
Experience?
ArtLibs is a collaboration between the Hood
Museum of Art and Dartmouth Colleges
Department of Psychological and Brain
Sciences investigating how people arrive
at their own understandings of art.
Using the ArtLibs app, we invite you to
share your interpretations of a selection of
artworks on view at the Hood Museum.
If you are comfortable, you can opt in to
have your anonymous responses saved for
research purposes. In this case, you will be
compensated for your time. Please
download the app for more information.

INTERPRETATION STYLE
Interested in
Participating?
1) Download the ArtLibs app
2) Select a work of art from the numbered
options on the apps museum map and
head to its location.
3) Provide your interpretation of the art by
selecting it on the museum map and
pressing the Interpret button. The
on-screen instructions will guide you
through the rest! Please do not read the
object label until you have completed the
full interpretation task.
4) When your museum visit is over, press
the End Session button (found below the
museum map) to view your visit summary,
which includes information about your
museum personality, how you compare to
other visitors, and more.
